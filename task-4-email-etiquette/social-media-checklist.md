# Social Media Do's and Don'ts for Students

### ✅ The Do's
* **Privacy Check:** Regularly audit your privacy settings to control who sees your posts.
* **Think Before Posting:** Ask yourself if you'd be okay with a future employer seeing the content.
* **Give Credit:** Always tag or credit original creators when sharing their work.
* **Constructive Engagement:** Use your platform to highlight projects, hackathons, or learning milestones.
* **Verify Info:** Check the facts before sharing news to prevent the spread of misinformation.

### ❌ The Don'ts
* **Over-Sharing:** Avoid posting sensitive personal info like your registration number, phone number, or home address.
* **Reactive Posting:** Never post while angry; digital footprints are permanent even if you delete the post later.
* **Ignore Terms of Service:** Don't skip reading the basic rules of the platforms you use.
* **Cyberbullying:** Never engage in or support online harassment or "trolling."
* **Plagiarism:** Don't claim someone else's digital work or code as your own.
