# Task 1: Digital Literacy Awareness Infographic

### Project Overview
As a Student Digital Ambassador, I designed this infographic to serve as a foundational resource for first-year students at VIT Bhopal. The goal is to simplify complex digital concepts into actionable steps for academic and professional success.

### Design Description
This visual resource covers three critical areas of digital literacy:
1.  **Defining Digital Literacy:** Moving beyond basic device usage to critical evaluation of online information.
2.  **Essential Tools:** Highlighting platforms like GitHub and LinkedIn that are vital for B.Tech students.
3.  **Cyber Hygiene:** Providing quick tips on 2FA and safe browsing habits.

### Reflection
Creating this infographic was an insightful exercise in visual communication. I found it particularly interesting to distill technical security advice into short, punchy bullet points that a busy student would actually read. The most challenging part was choosing a color palette that felt professional yet engaging for my peers.
